Advancing the Frontiers of Astroparticle Physics

CAPA promotes cutting-edge research in high-energy physics, nuclear physics, and astroparticle phenomena, unlocking the mysteries of the universe through scientific excellence and innovation.

Research Areas

Our multidisciplinary approach encompasses theoretical and experimental research in the most fundamental aspects of physics and cosmology.

Dark Matter Research

Investigating the nature of dark matter through advanced particle detection methods and quantum technologies in our DarkQuantum project.

Learn more

Cosmic Ray Physics

Studying ultra-high-energy cosmic messengers and their origins to understand the most energetic phenomena in the universe.

Learn more

Astroparticle Cosmology

Exploring the connection between particle physics and cosmology, including studies of the dark universe and fundamental constants.

Learn more
