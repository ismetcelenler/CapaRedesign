About CAPA

The Center for Astroparticles and High Energy Physics (CAPA) is a University Research Institute at the University of Zaragoza, officially approved by the Governing Council in 2024.

Our fundamental objective is to promote research in high-energy physics, nuclear physics, and astroparticle physics, tackling some of the most profound questions about our universe.

Through international collaborations and cutting-edge research projects like DarkQuantum, we contribute to the global effort to understand dark matter, cosmic phenomena, and fundamental particle interactions.
